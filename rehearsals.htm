Rehearsals

ULSO rehearsals take place on Tuesday evenings between 19:00 and 22:00 at St Stephen's Church, Gloucester Road, SW7 4RL. There may also be additional rehearsals on weekends in the lead-up to concerts.

If you are absent for a rehearsal, you must provide a dep and let the Orchestral Manager know at orchestral.manager@ulso.co.uk. Players are reminded that by joining ULSO, they have agreed to adhere to our depping policy, which may be viewed here.
